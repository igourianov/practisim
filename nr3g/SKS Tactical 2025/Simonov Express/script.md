# Simonov Express

### Start
* SKS loaded to capacity. Buttstock on hip.
* Shooter standing behind the Vtac

### Procedure
Shooter engage 5 IPSC metric targets through Vtac inner port of choice. \
Shooter must not shoot any given target through the same port more than once. \
25 seconds time limit.

### Scoring
5 targets, unlimited hits. \
Points for each hit:
* headbox: 5
* alpha: 4
* charlie: 3
* delta: 1
